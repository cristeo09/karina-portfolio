I’m Karina, a wedding and portrait photographer with a profound love for genuine human connection. For over a decade, I have been documenting love stories across the globe, translating fleeting emotions into timeless, monochromatic art. My approach is rooted in photojournalism—stepping back to let your day unfold naturally, while quietly capturing the honest, unscripted moments that matter most.

I believe that the most beautiful images are born from authenticity. Rather than relying on stiff poses, I focus on the quiet glances, the erupting laughter, and the raw vulnerability of two people committing their lives to one another. When I don't have a camera in hand, you can find me exploring vintage film archives or chasing the golden hour light in my hometown.
